Kilkenny

About Kilkenny

Kilkenny is a city in Kilkenny County, Ireland. It is home to the banks of the River Nore and the famed Kilkenny Castle. The outside of the castle boasts traditional medieval architecture in the heart of the city, right off of the main road. The interior of the castle itself has been preserved and restored to create an interactive exhibit for people to visit, and the grounds are a beautiful place to picnic or enjoy a sunny day.

Kilkenny has many festivals and cultural events, and embraces its medieval heritage. St. Canice's Cathedral and Round Tower is another popular attraction in Kilkenny. The architecture throughout the city, and anyone looking to grab a souvenir can stop by the Kilkenny Design Center to browse a large selection of art and souvenirs. On Saturday, there is a market on the main street in town where you can purchase different food and crafts.
