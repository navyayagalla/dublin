A Trip to the Countryside & Howth Cliffs

May 6, 2024 ~ Monday

Hooray for the first day of the trip! We went to Howth Cliffs straight from the Dublin airport to fight our looming jet lag. The cliffs were absolutely beautiful, right on the sea and surrounded by blooming yellow flowers. The steep stone steps led down to an incredible view. After exploring Howth and eating some lovely seafood at Crabby Jo's, we went back to Dublin and checked into our lodging for the trip. From there we enjoyed a dinner at the oldest pub in Ireland, the Brazen Head!

Exploring Dublin: A City of History and Culture

May 7, 2024 ~ Tuesday

On this Tuesday, we had our first day of orientation for the CEA Capa program and went on a historic bus tour of Dublin! Our tour guide showed us many of the famous battle locations while telling us about the bigger parts of Irish history. We learned more about the Great Famine of Ireland and how that led to Irish emigration across the world. Some of the locations we went to on this tour included the People's Gardens, The Sacred Heart Church which included a dedication to the buried rebels, the Jeanie Johnston Famine Ship & surrounding statues, and the Christchurch Cathedral.
