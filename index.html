About Me

Hi! My name is Navya, and I'm currently a student at the University of Michigan. I'm participating in a 3-week study abroad program in Dublin, Ireland, through the University of Michigan School of Information. We are learning about web accessibility and developing a website to document our travels. Over the course of these three weeks, we will have explored much of the Emerald Isle including Galway, Bray, Howth, Glendalough, Kilkenny, and Belfast. I'm excited to experience Irish culture, especially in relation to their history, literature, and music. Hopefully, after this program, I'll know what it means to feel the luck of the Irish! Éirinn go Brách!
